Our Story

Your Trusted Electrical Partner in Vadodara

TRUST MEE was founded with a simple mission: to provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services to the people of Vadodara. Over the past decade, we have grown from a small local service provider to one of the most trusted names in electrical solutions.

We are a trusted electrical service provider offering reliable solutions for both residential and commercial projects. With a strong focus on safety, quality, and customer satisfaction, our skilled technicians handle everything from new installations to repairs and maintenance.

In addition to electrical services, we specialize in dependable home appliance repair, ensuring your everyday appliances work efficiently and safely. Whether it's your home, office, or commercial space, we are committed to delivering timely service, honest pricing, and long-lasting results.
